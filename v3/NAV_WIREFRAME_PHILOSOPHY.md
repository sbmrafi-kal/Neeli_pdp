# Visible Wayfinding

Visible Wayfinding treats navigation as a calm map rather than a hidden utility. Every important destination remains present in the composition, but hierarchy is expressed through proportion, grouping and contrast instead of drawers or cryptic overflow controls. The visual language is direct, tactile and meticulously calibrated for a thumb-width screen.

Space is distributed with master-level precision. Labels are short because each word must earn its place; touch targets remain generous even when typography is quiet. Active state is communicated through one decisive line and a controlled colour shift, never through multiple badges or decorative noise.

The palette draws from the existing Kerala Ayurveda system: tulsi green provides continuity, milk-cream maintains readability, and restrained gold or rust marks the current position. The work should feel painstakingly crafted and mobile-native, with every alignment and interval refined as though it had been tested through countless real shopping sessions.

The composition values complete orientation over novelty. Trust destinations such as Reviews and FAQ are not buried, and decision destinations such as Science and Compare remain immediately reachable. The final canvas must look like the product of deep expertise: crisp, legible, balanced and intentionally free of accidental wrapping or clipped content.
